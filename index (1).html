System Architecture & Scoring Logic
How HireIQ works under the hood
Stage 1 — JD Parsing & Rubric Creation

The AI model reads the raw job description and extracts a structured scoring rubric: required skills, experience band, domain knowledge requirements, soft skills, and culture fit signals. This rubric becomes the basis for candidate evaluation.

Stage 2 — Candidate Match Scoring (0–100)

Each candidate's profile is compared against the rubric. Scoring dimensions: skill coverage (40%), experience fit (25%), domain relevance (20%), leadership/growth signals (15%). Every score includes a plain-English explanation for recruiter transparency.

Stage 3 — Interest Simulation & Scoring (0–100)

Simulated conversational outreach: HireIQ's AI agent plays the recruiter, asking about career goals, role excitement, current situation, and availability. It analyses response sentiment, specificity, and enthusiasm to derive an Interest Score — modeling what a real engagement conversation would reveal.

Stage 4 — Combined Ranking & Shortlist

Combined Score = 0.6 × Match + 0.4 × Interest. The final ranked output gives recruiters a single, actionable shortlist with full audit trail — no black boxes.
